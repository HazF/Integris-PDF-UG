Zooming in to Areas of Interest

Location    
GUI or Keyboard (Power spectrum only) Right click and select Enable Zoom, then drag to select the area of interest  
Classic menu Analyze > Graph > Enable Zoom
Modern menu Graph > Enable Zoom

Integris 2000 has the facility to zoom in to areas of interest on a power spectrum graph. This can be useful when you want to find exact frequencies or amplitudes of signals on the graph.

NOTE: To use this feature you must have Zoom enabled. Zoom is only available when displaying Spectrum graphs.

To zoom into an area on a spectrum graph:

  1. Ensure Zoom is enabled, by either:
    • right clicking on a power spectrum graph and selecting Enable Zoom, or
    • On Classic menus, click on Analyze, then open the Graph menu. Then select Enable Zoom, or
    • On Modern menus, click on the Graph menu and select Enable Zoom

  2. Select the area of interest by either:

    • Depressing the left mouse button then dragging the mouse over the area you want to examine, or

    • Using the touch screen, drag finger or stylus over the area you want to examine (See Fig 1.1 )

    Selecting an area of the graph

  3. A magnified area of the spectrum will appear allowing you to set markers or use the on-screen amplitude and frequency values.

To disable Zoom and return to full screen:

  1. Right click on the screen , or touch it with your finger or stylus.
  2. Click or press Unzoom
